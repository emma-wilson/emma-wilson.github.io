## About Me

Welcome! I am a PhD student in the [CAMARADES](https://www.ed.ac.uk/clinical-brain-sciences/research/camarades) Research Group at the [University of Edinburgh](https://www.ed.ac.uk/).

I specialise in the meta-research of preclinical data. I am currently supervised by [Dr Emily Sena](https://www.ed.ac.uk/profile/dr-emily-sena) and [Prof Peter Kind](https://www.ed.ac.uk/discovery-brain-sciences/our-staff/research-groups/peter-kind). My PhD project is funded by the [Simons Initiative for the Developing Brain](https://www.sidb.org.uk/).

I am passionate about open and reproducible research, and enjoy engaging the public with my research.
