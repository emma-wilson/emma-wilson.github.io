## Research interests

Previously, I have worked on systematic reviews of experimental neuroscience research. This led to a deep passion in research culture, research support, and researcher development. My current interests are:

- research culture change initiatives and community-led open research initiatives centred around equity, diversity, inclusion & accessibility and capacity building
- feminist and decolonial perspectives on open research, knowledge dissemination, and information retrieval
- critical disability studies perspectives on neuroscience (especially neurodivergent experiences in academia and education)
